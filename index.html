About me

Hello! My name is Hisham Bin Ab Rahim. I'm a 21 years old full-stack developer based in Malaysia. My goal is to be a skilled full-stack developer and to create many great things. During my spare time I work on projects, watch web development conferences, anime and several other shows! I also like to chat with people and play games but I always try my best to put my time on learning coding and other things from Websites like Coursera and great resources listed in Reddit.

The websites that I develop are responsive, built to be search engine friendly and follow the latest W3C web standards guidelines. They are supported by JQuery, AJAX, MySQL and PHP for interactivity and functionality. In order to complement them, I use flat designs and Google material design's principles.
